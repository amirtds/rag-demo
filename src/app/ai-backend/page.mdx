# AI Backend

In this section we see how the AI backend helps us to generate the course content

## Content Embedding

In this step we provide the source content, and the system will analyze the content and embed it into a language model.
This process ensures that the responses and practice questions you receive are directly based on the material,
providing an accurate and insightful learning experience without any fabricated information.
